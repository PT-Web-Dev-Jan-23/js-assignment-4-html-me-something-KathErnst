My Family

Nate

I met my husband, Nate, about 8 years ago. Our mutual friend suggested many times that we should date. One day when he and some friends came over to my apartment, I cooked a pizza and set off the smoke detector. I propped open the door off the side of the kitchen, only to realize about an hour later that my roommate's cat "Puttu" was missing.

After searching down the streets and alley surrounding my building, we gave it up as a lost cause. I tried to think about the best way I could apologize to my roommate for having lost her cat. Then Nate called me. He said he saw a cat that looked exactly like Puttu run across the street, and he was following it. I joined him and we went on a two-hour long adventure through my South City neighborhood, trying to catch this cat.

When it finally ran under a wood fence, we could not follow anymore. I trudged back to my apartment and found my roommate sitting on the couch with Puttu. Nate and I had been chasing a stray around the neighborhood the entire time! We started dating about a week later.
